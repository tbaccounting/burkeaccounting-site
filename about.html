How We Work

1. Intake & Estimate

You share a quick overview of your situation and upload or list your expected forms. BurkeAccounting responds with a ballpark fee and document checklist.

2. Document Upload

You securely upload files using your own Google Drive or Dropbox links, or share via another agreed-upon method.

3. Draft & Review

Your return is prepared, and you receive a summary to review before filing. Once approved and paid, your return is submitted and a copy is provided for your records.
