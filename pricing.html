Pricing

Transparent pricing with a simple structure and a firm cap.

How BurkeAccounting Pricing Works

Every individual tax return follows the same basic idea:

  1. Start with a base price. Simple W-2 only returns start at $20.
  2. Add a small percentage of your tax outcome. This applies whether you receive a refund or owe taxes.
  3. Adjust for complexity when needed. More forms and more time mean a higher fee.
  4. Apply the hard cap. No matter how complex your return becomes, you never pay more than $270 for a single return.

Example Pricing Scenarios

Simple W-2 Return

  • One or two W-2s
  • Standard deduction
  • Basic credits only

Typical range: $20 – $60

Actual fee depends on time involved and final tax outcome, but always starts with a $20 base and stays well below the $270 cap.

Side-Gig / Self-Employed

  • W-2 plus 1099-NEC / 1099-K income
  • Basic expense tracking
  • Schedule C preparation

Typical range: $80 – $200

Includes higher complexity and additional time to organize income and expenses, still capped at $270 total.

Complex Return (Cap Applies)

  • Multiple Schedules and 1099s
  • Rental properties and investments
  • Self-employment and itemized deductions

Max $270

No matter how many qualifying forms are included, your tax prep fee for a single return will not exceed $270.
